Spanish Sessions

Our Spanish suppport coaches can help you with writing, reading, and speaking in Spanish. They will not, however, proofread or edit your work. Please be aware and responsible for your own professor's policies on what sort of help is allowed or not allowed in your class, if applicable.

The Spanish support coaches are excited to teach you strategies to help you further your language development.
